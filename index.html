Meet Your Consultant

Gurvinder Singh

Hi! I'm Gurvinder Singh — a Regulated Canadian Immigration Consultant and the founder of 5K Immigration Services. Before this, I worked in software engineering. That's not a side note — it's how I approach every file. I build applications the way I used to build systems: documented, tested, and ready to hold up under scrutiny.

I work with individuals and families coming to Canada to study, work, or stay permanently. If you've had a refusal, I'll tell you exactly why it happened and whether there's a path forward. If you're starting fresh, we'll map out the right stream and set clear expectations before any application begins.
